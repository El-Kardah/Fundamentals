School & Bike Network Accessibility

What Philadelphia School District schools both enroll more than 1,200 students and are within 0.1 mile of the bike network?

Project Overview

This GIS analysis explores school accessibility and transportation equity in Philadelphia. Using enrollment data and the city’s bike network, this project identifies large district schools that are well connected to bicycle infrastructure. These locations may represent opportunities for improved student mobility, safety planning, and sustainable transportation investment.

Breaking Down the Problem

The What: Philadelphia School District schools

  1. Identify schools with enrollment greater than 1,200 students
  2. From that selection, identify schools located within 0.1 mile of the bike network

GIS Methods Used

  • Select by Attributes to identify district schools with enrollment over 1,200
  • Select by Location to find schools within 0.1 mile of the bike network
  • Subset Selection to refine results from the initial attribute query

Results

Solution: Five schools across four neighborhoods met both criteria.

  • Central High School — Logan
  • Solis-Cohen, Solomon School — Oxford Circle
  • Anne Frank Elementary School — Bustleton
  • Baldi Middle School — Bustleton
  • Frankford High School — Frankford
